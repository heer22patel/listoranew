What is Transaction Coordination?

Transaction Coordination is the management of all administrative tasks involved in a real estate transaction from contract to closing.

At Listora Digital Media, we handle the paperwork, deadlines, communication, and compliance processes so real estate professionals can focus on serving clients and closing more deals. We ensure every transaction moves smoothly, efficiently, and on schedule.

Step 02

Key deadlines and milestones are established and communicated to all parties to ensure everyone is aligned from the start.
