What is CRM Management & Automation?

CRM Management & Automation is the process of implementing, configuring, and optimizing customer relationship management systems to organize data, streamline workflows, and automate the repetitive tasks that slow businesses down.

At Listora Digital Media, we build CRM systems that work the way your business works — with automated follow-ups, structured pipelines, and organized customer data — so your team can focus on generating revenue instead of managing systems manually.

A well-implemented CRM doesn't just organize data. It creates the foundation for consistent communication, faster follow-ups, and scalable growth.

    Our CRM Management & Automation Process

    A clear 6-step process — from understanding your business to ongoing growth support — designed to build a CRM that actually works for your team.

    From Business Assessment to Automated Growth — Fast.

    Customized, scalable, and built for your workflow

    Step 01

    Business Assessment

    We evaluate your current processes, tools, and requirements — understanding your sales workflow, customer journey, and the specific challenges your team faces before building anything.

    Step 02

    CRM Strategy

    A customized CRM structure is planned around your business — pipeline stages, contact organization, automation logic, and integration requirements are all mapped out before any setup begins.

    Step 03

    Setup & Integration

    The CRM is configured and connected — your tools integrated, contacts imported, pipelines structured, and the system built to reflect the way your team actually works.

    Step 04

    Automation Implementation

    Workflows and automations are created — email sequences, task triggers, follow-up reminders, and pipeline automations all built to reduce manual effort and keep your business moving.

    Step 05

    Testing & Optimization

    Performance is reviewed and refined — automations tested, workflows adjusted, and the system optimized to ensure everything runs as intended before full deployment.

    Step 06

    Ongoing Growth Support

    Continuous improvements ensure long-term success — as your business grows and your needs evolve, we update and expand your CRM to keep it working at its best.
